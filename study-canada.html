Why Study in Canada?

Based on a globally recognized, stable education system, Canada offers a wide range of electives, rich activities, and vibrant clubs,
making it a safe yet attractive study destination for international students and their families.

Main Advantages of Studying in Canada
North American Standard English

Clear pronunciation and an internationally recognized English learning environment.

Safe and Systematic Education System

Guarantees stability not only in academics but in overall life.

ELL (English Learning Support)

Support for non-native speakers to adapt quickly.

A Truly Global Environment

Multicultural experiences with students from over 100 countries.

Inclusive Society

A culture of low discrimination and respect for diversity.

Affordable Study Costs

More economical tuition and living expenses compared to other English-speaking countries.

Free Public Education

Children can study tuition-free in public schools if a parent is enrolled in a government-designated university or holds a work permit.

Parent Accompaniment Visa

Visitor visa granted to parents of minor international students.

Simplified Visa Process

Mainly document-based screening without in-person interviews.

Close to Korea

Direct flight Incheon–Vancouver is about 10 hours.

Vancouver, the Best Choice for Study Abroad

With mild climate, beautiful nature, and a pleasant living environment, Vancouver is considered one of the most livable cities in Canada. Metro Vancouver consists of Burnaby, Coquitlam, Surrey, Langley, Maple Ridge, etc., and each school district independently operates its own international student program.
