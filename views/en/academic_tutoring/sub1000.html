Academic Tutoring

HERALD Canada-Specific Academic Tutoring Program

HIS Herald Education Center offers specialized academic programs for international students in Canada, organized by level:
Elementary, Secondary, Post-Secondary, and EC (Extracurricular Activity) programs.

HERALD’s personalized tutoring programs are designed to reflect each student’s subject level, school curriculum, learning comprehension, and study pace.
Students can selectively enroll in the areas where they need the most support.
