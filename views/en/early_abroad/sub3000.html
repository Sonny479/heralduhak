Why St. George’s School
1. Urban Boarding in the Heart of Vancouver

Located in one of Vancouver’s most prestigious and safest residential areas, St. George’s offers an urban boarding experience where students engage in academics, athletics, and cultural activities within a vibrant and international city.

2. A Premier Boys’ Education

Founded in 1930, the school provides a specialized curriculum and mentorship system tailored to the growth, learning needs, and developmental stages of boys.

3. A Century of Community and Tradition

With nearly 100 years of history, the school boasts a strong alumni network, deep school spirit, and a tradition of leadership development and community values.

4. A Place for Endless Exploration

Students can pursue their passions through more than 40 athletic teams, 50+ clubs, and 24 AP courses, allowing each student to explore their interests and maximize their potential.

Boarding Admissions at St. George’s School

Admission to the boarding program is highly competitive and holistic, assessing academic strength, character, leadership potential, interview performance, and personal qualities.Accurate information and professional preparation are essential.

The Seoul headquarters of Herald Uhak Canada HIS-Herald Education Center is an Authorized Representative of St. George’s School, supporting students in Korea, China and Canada throughout the entire application process —from school selection to documentation, testing, and interview preparation.

Admissions Consultation

Families interested in applying to St. George’s boarding program are encouraged to book a consultation with our Authorized Representative, Executive Director Julia Park.

Our Support Includes:
  • Academic profile & course-level assessment
  • Boarding suitability review
  • Application strategy and timeline by grade
  • Required documents checklist
  • Interview preparation for students and parents
  • Guidance on test selection (SSAT / CAT4 / DET) and diagnostic evaluation
Final Review & Admission Decision

After the full application—documents, tests, and interviews—is completed, the school conducts its final review.
Upon acceptance, students receive support regarding dormitory placement, course selection, arrival planning, and orientation.

Herald Uhak Canada and HIS Herald Education Center continue supporting students even after enrollment to ensure a smooth transition into boarding life.
