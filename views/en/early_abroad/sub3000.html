Why St. George’s School
1. Urban Boarding in the Heart of Vancouver

Located in one of Vancouver’s most prestigious and safest residential areas, St. George’s offers an urban boarding experience where students engage in academics, athletics, and cultural activities within a vibrant and international city.

2. A Premier Boys’ Education

Founded in 1930, the school provides a specialized curriculum and mentorship system tailored to the growth, learning needs, and developmental stages of boys.

3. A Century of Community and Tradition

With nearly 100 years of history, the school boasts a strong alumni network, deep school spirit, and a tradition of leadership development and community values.

4. A Place for Endless Exploration

Students can pursue their passions through more than 40 athletic teams, 50+ clubs, and 24 AP courses, allowing each student to explore their interests and maximize their potential.
