About Us

About Us - 

Preparing Your Child’s Future with Tailored Study Abroad Programs

Since 2006, HERALD Uhak Canada has drawn on its extensive global experience in study abroad and education consulting to guide students and families toward success.
From early study abroad to high school graduation and admissions to top universities, we provide personalized roadmaps that match each student’s goals and strengths.

Why Choose  ?

Practical and safe study abroad planning tailored to the student’s goals, aptitude, and family circumstances

◈ Personalized School Placement

We recommend the most suitable schools for each student’s aspirations and aptitudes, including public schools, Catholic private schools, and boarding schools.

◈ 20 Years of Proven Managed Study Abroad

With two decades of expertise, we ensure student safety and growth through structured, 24-hour academic and lifestyle support.

◈ Cultural & Language Integration Programs

From safe homestays (both Canadian and Korean host families) to diverse local activities, we help students adapt to Canadian culture and language while developing a global mindset.

◈ Expert University Admissions Consulting

Starting with Canada and extending to the U.S., U.K., Australia, Singapore, Japan, and Korea, our expert team supports admissions to top universities worldwide.

- Extracurricular Activities & Herald Student Reporters Program
- Research Projects with Distinguished University Professors

◈ Consulting for Returnee Students

For students returning to Korea after short-term study abroad
(within 3 years), we provide specialized guidance for admission into foreign schools(YISS, SFS, SIS, KIS etc.), Songdo/Jeju international schools, and other international schools in Seoul’s Gangnam district. Our flagship program, operated for 20 years by HIS Herald Education Center in Seoul, offers tailored tutoring and test preparation to minimize academic gaps and maximize admission success.
